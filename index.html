About This Tracker

The FIFA World Cup 2026 is the biggest football tournament in history, featuring 48 national teams competing across 16 host cities in Canada, Mexico, and the United States. The tournament runs from June 11 to July 19, 2026, with 104 matches spread across 12 group-stage groups followed by a knockout bracket from the Round of 32 through to the Final.

This tracker provides live match scores, real-time standings, an interactive knockout bracket, a full match schedule in your local timezone, a top scorers leaderboard, and an interactive venue map showing all 16 stadiums. Match data is updated automatically throughout the tournament.
