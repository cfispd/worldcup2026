This Privacy Policy describes how the FIFA World Cup 2026 Tracker ("this site", "we", "us") collects and uses information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data practices of this site.

1. Information We Collect

This site does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information. The only data collected is through the third-party analytics and advertising services described below.

We may automatically collect non-personal technical information including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website address
  • General geographic location (country/region level)

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (property ID: G-YJS6015CH2) to understand how visitors interact with this site. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data through cookies and similar technologies.

Information collected by Google Analytics may include pages you visit, time spent, and how you arrived at the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. This site uses the following types of cookies:

  • Functional cookies – We store your language preference (English or Chinese) and theme preference (light or dark) in your browser's localStorage so the site remembers your settings between visits.
  • Analytics cookies – Set by Google Analytics to track anonymous usage statistics as described above.
  • Advertising cookies – Set by Google AdSense to show relevant advertisements as described above.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this site.

5. Third-Party Services

This site integrates content from the following third-party services:

  • flagcdn.com – Provides country flag images. No personal data is shared with this service.
  • Leaflet / OpenStreetMap – Powers the interactive venue map. Map tile requests are made to OpenStreetMap servers. See OSM Privacy Policy.

  • Booking.com / Trip.com – Hotel and flight search links are provided via affiliate programs. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

This site does not store any personal data on its own servers. Analytics data retained by Google Analytics follows Google's standard data retention policies (default 14 months).

7. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.
